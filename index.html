How it works

Hate cooking? We’re for you.

01

Call us anytime

We are open 24/7 to serve you. Don't want to travel? That's fine. Just call us, order your favourite dishes and wait for us in the comfort of your own place. We deliver fresh meals on time within the city limits only.

02

Takeaway

In order to use the feature, you will need to place an order online and then head to the restaurant while the food gets prepared. Once you reach the restaurant, you will need to show the code to pick up your order.

03

Dine Out

We offer dine out services to our patrons 24/7 subject to the availability of seats. The small-town ambience, freshly cooked meals, wide-range of regional cuisines, pocket-friendly costs and a professional staff justify every reason you want to be here.
